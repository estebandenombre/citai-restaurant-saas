## 9) FAQ

- `Section 05 — Asked`
- `You’ll probably ask.`
- `Couldn’t find your question? Write to a real person at hello@tably.digital.`

Preguntas:

1. `Do you take a cut of my orders?`
   - `Never. Tably charges a flat monthly fee. Every euro a guest pays goes to you (minus payment processor fees, which we don’t touch).`
2. `What hardware do I need?`
   - `Whatever you already have. A phone for the host, a tablet on the pass, an old laptop in the office. Bring your own printers — we speak ESC/POS.`
3. `What happens if my Wi-Fi dies?`
   - `Service keeps running. Tably caches the active session locally and syncs the moment you’re back online. The till never closes.`
4. `Can I import my current menu?`
   - `Yes. Send us a PDF, a photo, even a hand-written list. Our team formats it for free during onboarding.`
5. `Where is my data stored?`
   - `Inside the EU on AWS Frankfurt. You can export everything as CSV any day, no questions asked, no fee.`
